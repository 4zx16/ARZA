❓ FAQ

Q: Can I install Linux instead of Windows?
A: Yes. All ARZA systems support Linux and dual-boot setups. But we only ship systems with a pre-installed OS — either Windows 11 Pro or Linux Mint. No barebones builds.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Most systems ship within 7–14 business days after confirming your build. Custom loops or high-end parts may add time — we’ll let you know if there’s a delay.

Q: Can I request specific brands for parts?
A: Definitely. Just include your preferences during your build submission. We'll honor them based on availability and budget.

Q: What's covered under warranty?
A: Everything — CPU, GPU, RAM, SSDs, fans, etc. — is covered under a 2-year warranty (parts & labor). But if there’s **clear, intentional damage**, tampering, or abuse, warranty is void. We don’t fix sabotage.

Q: What if my PC is damaged during shipping?
A: We don’t control third-party shipping. If there’s visible damage on delivery, file a claim directly with the shipping provider or retailer (if not bought through us). We pack tight — but accidents happen. Document it fast.

Q: My warranty is still active. What do I need for a repair?
A: Email us first. Then send us the full setup — charger, the device, and any essentials it needs to run. Missing items = possible delays or limitations in repairs.

Q: Warranty’s expired. Am I out of luck?
A: Not at all. We offer out-of-warranty repairs at a fair price. You'll just cover parts and labor. Reach out, and we’ll get you sorted.
